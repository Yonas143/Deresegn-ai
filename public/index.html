What is Deresegn AI?

Deresegn AI is your personal AI assistant that helps you manage expenses, scan receipts, and track spending - all through Telegram. No apps to download, no complicated setup. Just send a photo and let AI do the rest.

📸

Smart Receipt Scanning

Take a photo of any receipt and our AI instantly extracts merchant, amount, date, and category. Works with receipts in any language!

💬

Natural Language Queries

Ask questions like "How much did I spend this week?" or "Show me my coffee expenses" - get instant answers.

📊

Expense Analytics

Get detailed insights into your spending patterns. Track by category, merchant, or time period.

🔍

Smart Search

Find any receipt instantly. Search by merchant name, amount, date, or category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I deploy my own instance?

Yes! ScanLogic is open source. Visit github.com/yoniapp/Deresegn-ai for deployment instructions. Deploy to Render, Railway, or any Node.js hosting platform.

Is Deresegn AI free to use?

Yes! Deresegn AI is completely free to use. We may introduce premium features in the future, but core functionality will always remain free.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information with third parties. You can delete your data anytime.

What languages are supported?

ScanLogic can read receipts in any language! Our AI is trained on multilingual data and works with receipts from around the world.

Can I use it for business expenses?

Yes! Deresegn AI is perfect for business expense tracking. Export reports in Excel format for your accountant or tax filing.

Do I need to download an app?

No! Deresegn AI works entirely through Telegram. If you have Telegram, you're ready to go. No additional apps needed.
